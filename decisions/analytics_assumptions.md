# Analytics Assumptions

## Late Delivery Definition
A delivery is considered late if it occurs more than 7 days after shipment.
The dataset does not include promised delivery dates.

## Demand Volatility
Demand volatility is calculated using coefficient of variation on monthly
aggregated order quantities.

## Discounts
DiscountApplied is interpreted as a fractional value (e.g. 0.08 = 8%).
